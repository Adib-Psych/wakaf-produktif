Dua keranjang ceri kopi, dipetik di jam yang sama, dari pohon yang sama. Keranjang A langsung dibawa ke pulper dalam tiga jam. Keranjang B dibiarkan menumpuk di kebun hingga sore, baru diangkut malam hari. Saat di-cup berbulan-bulan kemudian: keranjang A memberikan profil bersih, manis, beraroma buah; keranjang B memberikan rasa fermentasi liar, off-flavor, asam tajam. Sumber buah sama, panen sama hari — tetapi jam yang berbeda menghasilkan kopi yang fundamentally berbeda.

Two baskets of coffee cherries, picked at the same hour, from the same tree. Basket A goes straight to the pulper within three hours. Basket B is left heaped in the garden until evening, transported only at night. When cupped months later: basket A yields a clean, sweet, fruity profile; basket B yields wild fermentation, off-flavors, sharp acidity. Same source, same harvest day — but different hours produce coffees that are fundamentally different.

Setelah Modul O mengunci buah mana yang dipetik (hanya merah penuh), modul ini menjawab pertanyaan berikutnya: berapa lama buah boleh menunggu sebelum diproses. Jawaban SOP Kementan dan praktik specialty di seluruh dunia konvergen pada satu angka: 12 jam. Lebih dari itu, mutu cup turun secara terukur — bukan karena ceri "rusak" secara visual, tetapi karena kimia internal biji sudah berubah.

After Module O locked in which fruit to pick (only fully red), this module answers the next question: how long can fruit wait before processing. The answer that Indonesian SOP and global specialty practice converge upon: 12 hours. Beyond that, cup quality drops measurably — not because the cherry "spoils" visually, but because the bean's internal chemistry has already shifted.

Modul ini ditulis oleh seorang psikolog klinis yang sedang belajar agronomi kopi dari awal. Pendekatannya: meminjam konsep therapeutic window dari kedokteran gawat darurat. Stroke iskemik akut: rt-PA harus diberikan dalam window 4,5 jam — lebih dari itu, kerusakan otak menjadi permanen. Sepsis: 1-hour bundle (kultur darah, antibiotik, fluid) — setiap jam keterlambatan menaikkan mortalitas 7-8%. Trauma: golden hour — penanganan dalam jam pertama menentukan survival. Kopi tidak menyangkut nyawa, tetapi mekanisme waktu-kritisnya identik.

This module is written by a clinical psychologist learning coffee agronomy from scratch. The approach: borrow therapeutic window from emergency medicine. Acute ischemic stroke: rt-PA must be given within a 4.5-hour window — beyond that, brain damage becomes permanent. Sepsis: 1-hour bundle (blood culture, antibiotics, fluids) — every hour of delay raises mortality 7-8%. Trauma: golden hour — care in the first hour determines survival. Coffee is not life-or-death, but the time-critical mechanism is identical.

Konteks Kebun Wakaf NgantangThe Ngantang Waqf Garden Context

Kebun kopi wakaf di Ngantang berada pada lereng dengan kemiringan yang nyata — terutama di Bidang 3 (yang paling miring). Saat hujan deras turun (2.500–3.000 mm setahun), tanah dengan kemiringan ini mudah mengalami surface run-off: air mengalir di permukaan, membawa pupuk yang ditabur ke arah lembah, jauh dari pohon yang membutuhkan. Topografi ini menambah satu alasan struktural lagi mengapa pupuk harus masuk ke dalam tanah via parit, bukan tertinggal di permukaan.

The Ngantang waqf garden sits on a noticeably sloping terrain — especially Block 3 (the steepest). When heavy rains fall (2,500–3,000 mm per year), soil with this slope is prone to surface run-off: water flows across the surface, carrying surface-broadcast fertilizer toward the valley, away from the trees that need it. This topography adds one more structural reason why fertilizer must enter the soil via a trench, not be left on the surface.

⚠ Realitas Saat Ini⚠ Current Reality

Di Ngantang, eksekusi lapangan dilakukan oleh Pak Kumpul sebagai pekerja Bagian Bawah Pohon (yang sebelumnya juga membangun rorak — Modul L). Aplikasi pupuk parit lingkar piringan memerlukan: cangkul atau garpu tanah, pengukur (langkah kaki cukup), wadah pupuk, dan disiplin untuk konsisten radius dan kedalaman di semua pohon. Untuk 750 pohon × dua aplikasi setahun, total ada 1.500 kali "buka-tutup parit" — pekerjaan yang serius dan layak dianggarkan tersendiri di RAB.

In Ngantang, field execution is carried out by Pak Kumpul as the Below-Tree worker (who has also built the rorak conservation pits — Module L). Trench-application fertilization requires: a hoe or garden fork, a measure (foot-paces will do), a fertilizer container, and the discipline to maintain consistent radius and depth across all trees. For 750 trees × two applications per year, that means a total of 1,500 trench "open-and-close" cycles — serious work that deserves its own budget line in the RAB.

Implikasinya: cara aplikasi bukan detail kosmetik melainkan multiplier dari investasi pupuk. Pupuk Rp 27 juta setahun (RAB Siklus 2027) yang ditabur tebar di permukaan dapat memberi hasil yang sebanding dengan setengah dosis tetapi diaplikasikan benar. Sebaliknya: dosis benar + aplikasi benar = nilai investasi penuh tercapai. Modul ini sangat penting di-cross-check oleh Wakif sebelum eksekusi aplikasi pertama, supaya tidak terjadi kebocoran nilai.

The implication: application technique is not a cosmetic detail but a multiplier of the fertilizer investment. An IDR 27 million annual fertilizer outlay (RAB Cycle 2027) broadcast on the surface can yield results comparable to half the dose applied correctly. Conversely: correct dose + correct application = full investment value realized. This Module deserves a careful cross-check from the Waqif before the first application, to prevent value leakage.

Teori Dasar: Anatomi Window 12 JamTheory: Anatomy of the 12-Hour Window

schema building

Mengapa 12 jam, bukan 24 jam atau "yang penting hari yang sama"? Karena begitu ceri dipetik dari pohon, jam biologisnya dimulai. Dalam ceri yang masih menempel di pohon, biji terlindung oleh metabolisme yang aktif: pohon menyuplai O₂, mengatur kelembapan, dan biji menjalankan respirasi dorman terkontrol. Saat dipetik, suplai ini terputus. Enzim di dalam biji terus bekerja tetapi tanpa kontrol — terutama enzim yang memecah gula dan asam organik. Mikroflora permukaan (ragi liar, bakteri lactic acid, bakteri acetic acid) mulai berkembang biak di kulit ceri yang masih basah. Setiap jam berikutnya, "interior" biji bergeser dari kondisi netral ke kondisi fermentasi tak terkontrol.

Anatomi window 12 jam dipecah jadi 4 fase kritis:

  • Jam 0 (petik) — ceri keluar dari pohon. Suhu interior ~25-28°C, pH netral, mikroflora masih dorman. Tidak ada perubahan rasa yang terdeteksi.
  • Jam 2 (turun titik kumpul) — batas aman pertama. Ceri masih bisa menerima panas matahari ringan tetapi sudah harus pindah ke tempat teduh. Akumulasi panas di tumpukan mulai bermasalah.
  • Jam 6 (titik kritis) — enzim pemecah gula mulai aktif, ragi liar mulai berkembang. Profil cup masih recoverable kalau langsung di-pulping, tetapi sudah dekat batas.
  • Jam 12 (batas mutlak) — di luar window ini, fermentasi tak terkontrol sudah signifikan. Buah masih bisa di-proses, tetapi profilnya sudah "fermenty" — bukan cacat fatal, tetapi bukan specialty.

Why 12 hours, not 24 hours or "same day"? Because the moment a cherry is picked from the tree, its biological clock starts. While still attached, the bean is protected by active tree metabolism: O₂ supply, controlled humidity, and dormant respiration. When picked, this supply ends. Enzymes inside the bean continue working but without control — especially enzymes that break down sugars and organic acids. Surface microflora (wild yeast, lactic-acid bacteria, acetic-acid bacteria) begin multiplying on the still-wet cherry skin. With each passing hour, the bean's "interior" shifts from a neutral state toward uncontrolled fermentation.

The anatomy of the 12-hour window breaks into 4 critical phases:

  • Hour 0 (pick) — cherry leaves the tree. Interior temp ~25-28°C, pH neutral, microflora still dormant. No detectable flavor change yet.
  • Hour 2 (move to collection point) — first safety threshold. Cherry can still take mild sun but must shift to shade. Heat accumulation in heaps starts being a problem.
  • Hour 6 (critical point) — sugar-breaking enzymes activate, wild yeasts begin multiplying. Cup profile is still recoverable if pulped immediately, but close to the edge.
  • Hour 12 (absolute limit) — beyond this, uncontrolled fermentation is significant. The fruit can still be processed, but the profile has shifted "fermenty" — not a fatal defect, but not specialty either.

Kenapa 12 jam menjadi tipping point, bukan 6 atau 18 jam? Karena pada kisaran 8-14 jam pasca-petik, populasi mikroba permukaan ceri mencapai threshold di mana mereka mulai menembus kulit ceri dan masuk ke lendir (mucilage) yang melekat pada biji. Sebelum threshold itu, mikroba terbatas di permukaan dan masih bisa dicuci hilang saat pulping/washing. Setelah threshold itu, mereka sudah internal — kontak dengan biji, mempengaruhi profil rasa yang akan tertinggal di green bean. Window 12 jam adalah median kompromi antara realitas lapangan (kebun jauh, ojek transport terbatas) dan window biologis yang sebenarnya 8-14 jam.

Why does 12 hours become the tipping point, not 6 or 18? Because in the 8-14 hour range post-pick, surface microbe populations on the cherry reach a threshold where they begin penetrating the cherry skin and entering the mucilage layer attached to the bean. Before that threshold, microbes are confined to the surface and can still be washed away during pulping. After it, they are already internal — in contact with the bean, affecting the flavor profile that will be retained in the green bean. The 12-hour window is the median compromise between field realities (remote gardens, limited transport) and the actual 8-14 hour biological window.

Analogi dari Kedokteran Gawat Darurat — Therapeutic Window & Golden HourAn Analogy from Emergency Medicine — Therapeutic Window & Golden Hour

Dalam kedokteran gawat darurat, ada beberapa kondisi di mana waktu adalah substrat utama intervensi — bukan obat, bukan dosis, bukan dokter. Stroke iskemik akut: rt-PA (tissue plasminogen activator) hanya boleh diberikan dalam window 4,5 jam dari onset gejala — lebih dari itu, kerusakan otak tidak reversibel meski obat diberikan. Sepsis: 1-hour bundle (Surviving Sepsis Campaign) — kultur darah, antibiotik spektrum luas, dan resusitasi cairan harus dimulai dalam 1 jam pertama; setiap jam keterlambatan menambah mortalitas 7-8%. Trauma berat: golden hour — penanganan dalam jam pertama menentukan survival rate. Pada ketiga kondisi ini, jam yang berjalan adalah variabel paling agresif.

In emergency medicine, several conditions exist where time itself is the primary substrate of intervention — not the drug, not the dose, not the clinician. Acute ischemic stroke: rt-PA (tissue plasminogen activator) may only be given within a 4.5-hour window from symptom onset — beyond that, brain damage becomes irreversible even with the drug. Sepsis: 1-hour bundle (Surviving Sepsis Campaign) — blood cultures, broad-spectrum antibiotics, and fluid resuscitation must begin within the first hour; every hour of delay adds 7-8% to mortality. Major trauma: the golden hour — care in the first hour determines survival rate. In all three, the running clock is the most aggressive variable.

Pasca-petik kopi mengikuti logika identik. Ceri yang sama, diproses via tiga timing berbeda, menghasilkan profil cup yang sangat berbeda: (1) pulping dalam 0-6 jam pasca-petik — biji bersih, profil cup mengikuti varietas + teruariasi (fully washed clean profile). (2) pulping dalam 6-12 jam — masih dalam window, profil tetap dominan varietas tetapi dengan layer fermentasi lembut yang banyak roaster justru cari. (3) pulping di luar 12 jam — fermentasi liar sudah signifikan, profil cup bergeser ke fermenty (kadang dicari sebagai "natural" tetapi BUKAN — natural process punya kontrol berbeda). Window 12 jam bukan trade-off kemudahan vs kualitas; window itu adalah batas biologis yang dapat diobati.

Coffee post-harvest follows identical logic. The same cherry, processed at three different timings, yields vastly different cup profiles: (1) pulping within 0-6 hours post-pick — clean beans, cup profile follows the varietal + terroir (fully washed clean profile). (2) pulping within 6-12 hours — still inside the window, profile remains varietal-dominant with a soft fermentation layer that many roasters actually seek. (3) pulping outside 12 hours — wild fermentation is significant, profile shifts to fermenty (sometimes confused with "natural" but it is NOT — natural process has different controls). The 12-hour window is not a convenience-vs-quality trade-off; it is the treatable biological boundary.

Pola yang muncul: empat sumber konvergen — window 12 jam adalah batas operasional yang sah. Bukan 24 jam, bukan "asal hari yang sama", bukan tergantung mood lapangan. Kementan 2019 memberikan angka paling presisi (12 jam), Wintgens memberi justifikasi teoritis (reception window 6-12 jam), BRIN melengkapi dengan data biokimia post-harvest, Puslitbang konvergen secara prinsip urgency. Tidak ada sumber yang kontradiktif — yang ada hanya tingkat presisi yang berbeda.

The emerging pattern: all four sources converge — the 12-hour window is the valid operational boundary. Not 24 hours, not "as long as same day", not based on field mood. Kementan 2019 provides the most precise figure (12 hours), Wintgens supplies the theoretical justification (6-12 hour reception window), BRIN supplements with post-harvest biochemistry data, Puslitbang converges in principle on urgency. No source is contradictory — only different levels of precision.

✓ Tiga Kesalahan Umum yang Menggagalkan Window 12 Jam✓ Three Common Mistakes that Derail the 12-Hour Window

(1) Ceri menumpuk terkena terik matahari langsung — Setiap kenaikan 10°C suhu interior tumpukan menggandakan laju reaksi enzimatik (aturan Q₁₀ kimia). Tumpukan ceri 30 menit di matahari = setara 1 jam di teduh. Solusi: keranjang dangkal, segera teduhkan, atau tutup terpal putih reflektif. (2) Wadah tertutup rapat dalam transport — Tumpukan ceri di karung plastik tertutup menghasilkan akumulasi panas + CO₂ + uap air. Ini kondisi ideal untuk fermentasi anaerob liar. Solusi: wadah berlubang/anyaman, jangan plastik tertutup. (3) Panen pagi tetapi transport sore — Common case: tim petik pagi sampai siang, lalu menunggu jemputan motor sampai sore. Total elapsed time bisa 8-10 jam sebelum tiba di pulper, plus 1-2 jam antrian pulping = di luar window. Solusi: jadwal jemputan motor harus reliable, atau pulper portabel di kebun.

(1) Cherries heaped in direct sunlight — Every 10°C rise in heap interior temperature doubles enzymatic reaction rate (Q₁₀ chemistry rule). A 30-minute heap in the sun = 1 hour in the shade. Solution: shallow baskets, immediate shading, or white reflective tarp cover. (2) Tightly sealed containers during transport — Cherries in closed plastic sacks accumulate heat + CO₂ + water vapor. Ideal conditions for wild anaerobic fermentation. Solution: ventilated/woven baskets, never sealed plastic. (3) Morning harvest but evening transport — Common case: pickers work morning to noon, then wait for motorcycle pickup until evening. Total elapsed time can reach 8-10 hours before arriving at the pulper, plus 1-2 hours queueing = outside the window. Solution: reliable motorcycle pickup schedule, or portable pulper in the garden.

Implikasi mutu: panen Kebun NGT proyeksi 750 kg green bean Siklus 2027. Specialty grade (window 12 jam dipatuhi) dengan offtaker Bonsari/Nomad menargetkan harga Rp 95-130rb/kg. Commodity grade (di luar window) terjual Rp 45-60rb/kg ke tengkulak lokal. Selisih nilai = Rp 50-70rb/kg × 750 kg ≈ Rp 37,5-52,5 juta hilang per siklus jika window 12 jam tidak dipatuhi. Investasi terbesar bukan di pulper atau dryer, melainkan di disiplin jadwal. Inilah mengapa Modul P (window) ditempatkan setelah Modul O (petik selektif) dan sebelum Modul Q (kadar air): tiga modul tidak bisa dipisah di lapangan.

Quality implication: NGT Garden's projected Cycle 2027 harvest is 750 kg green bean. Specialty grade (12-hour window honored) with Bonsari/Nomad offtakers targets IDR 95-130k/kg. Commodity grade (outside window) sells at IDR 45-60k/kg to local middlemen. Value differential = IDR 50-70k/kg × 750 kg ≈ IDR 37.5-52.5 million lost per cycle if the 12-hour window is not honored. The biggest investment is not the pulper or dryer, but schedule discipline. This is why Module P (window) sits between Module O (selective picking) and Module Q (moisture): the three modules cannot be separated in the field.

Langkah 2 · Transfer ke Titik Kumpul Setiap 2 JamStep 2 · Transfer to Collection Point Every 2 Hours
Tas pundak / keranjang dangkal yang dibawa pemetik DIKOSONGKAN ke wadah utama setiap 1-2 jam — JANGAN menunggu sampai penuh kalau itu artinya >2 jam terkumpul. Wadah utama harus di tempat TEDUH (di bawah pohon penaung / tarp putih reflektif). Tumpukan ceri MAKSIMAL 30 cm tinggi — di atas itu, panas akumulasi di interior naik signifikan (Q₁₀ rule). Tidak ada plastik tertutup, hanya wadah anyaman/berlubang. Picker shoulder bags / shallow baskets EMPTIED into the main container every 1-2 hours — DO NOT wait until full if that means >2 hours accumulated. Main container must be in SHADE (under shade tree / white reflective tarp). Cherry heap MAX 30 cm tall — above that, interior heat accumulation rises significantly (Q₁₀ rule). No sealed plastic, only woven/ventilated containers.
Langkah 3 · Sortasi Cepat di Titik KumpulStep 3 · Quick Sort at Collection Point
Sebelum dikirim, lakukan sortasi cepat di titik kumpul: buang ceri sakit (hitam, ada lubang PBKo, jamuran), buang daun + ranting yang terbawa. Sortasi sekunder ini hanya 5-10 menit per karung tapi mencegah cacat membawa fermentasi ke pulper. Catat berat ceri terbuang (untuk record OPT trend). Catatan: sortasi ukuran/density TIDAK dilakukan di sini — itu pulper job. Before dispatch, perform quick sort at collection point: discard diseased cherries (black, PBKo-bored, moldy), discard leaves + twigs that came along. This secondary sort takes 5-10 minutes per sack but prevents defective cherries from carrying fermentation into the pulper. Record discarded cherry weight (for OPT trend tracking). Note: size/density sorting is NOT done here — that's the pulper's job.
Langkah 4 · Transport ke Pulper — Single Trip ≤4 JamStep 4 · Transport to Pulper — Single Trip ≤4 Hours
Total durasi dari titik kumpul ke pulper (loading + perjalanan + unloading): MAKSIMAL 4 jam — termasuk antrian jika ada. Kalau dual-trip (pagi+siang), trip pertama berangkat jam 14:00 (ceri 06:00 → pulper 18:00 = 12 jam total). Trip kedua jam 16:00 (ceri 12:00 → pulper 20:00 = 8 jam total). Pickup truck/motor harus pakai bak terbuka dengan tarp putih (UV reflective), bukan box tertutup (akumulasi panas). Total duration from collection point to pulper (loading + travel + unloading): MAX 4 hours — including queue if any. For dual-trip (morning+noon), first trip departs 14:00 (cherry 06:00 → pulper 18:00 = 12 hours total). Second trip 16:00 (cherry 12:00 → pulper 20:00 = 8 hours total). Pickup truck/motorcycle must use open bed with white tarp (UV reflective), not closed box (heat accumulation).
Langkah 5 · Catat Window Aktual di Form Input — Per LotStep 5 · Record Actual Window in Form Input — Per Lot
Foto + catat di dashboard monitor-v2 Form Input untuk SETIAP lot harian: jam petik mulai, jam transfer ke titik kumpul, jam berangkat transport, jam tiba di pulper, jam selesai pulping. Hitung total elapsed time dan flag jika >12 jam. Data ini menjadi audit trail untuk Bonsari (mitra processing) dan input untuk evaluasi efisiensi siklus berikutnya. Photo + record in monitor-v2 dashboard Form Input for EACH daily lot: pick start time, transfer to collection time, transport depart time, arrival at pulper time, pulping completion time. Calculate total elapsed time and flag if >12 hours. This becomes the audit trail for Bonsari (processing partner) and input for next-cycle efficiency evaluation.
